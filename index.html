About Dr. Kitasho

Dr. Neema Kitasho is a lecturer at the Sokoine University of Agriculture, specializing in Environmental Sciences and Economics. With a PhD from the University of Geneva, Dr. Kitasho has extensive experience in interdisciplinary research, focusing on climate change and sustainable development.

Research

Dr. Kitasho's research interests include climate resilience, dryland ecosystems, and the economic impacts of environmental policies. Her work aims to develop sustainable solutions for environmental challenges in Tanzania and beyond.
